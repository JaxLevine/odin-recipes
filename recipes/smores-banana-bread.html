Smores Banana Bread

The best banana bread you'll every try.... With a twist!

Smores Banana Bread is a healthy dessert or breakfast option that's absolutely delicious and satisfies the strongest sugar cravings. It's a wonderful option for guests, with familiar smores flavors that bring about a sense of nostalgia!

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Maple syrup
  • Almond flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Graham crackers
  • Marshmallows

Instructions

  1. To begin making smores banana bread, preheat the oven to 350F and grease your bread pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas. Whisk in the eggs, then add the vanilla and maple syrup.
  3. Then, in a small bowl, mix together the almond flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet and stir to combine. Next, add in half of the chocolate chips.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased bread pan and top with chocolate chips, graham crackers, and marshmallows!
  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving, and enjoy!
